Skeena Region

  • Alaskan fisheries catch all species of salmon in the Skeena
  • Catch of Skeena sockeye is estimated through the Northern Boundary Run Reconstruction Model and can be very high in some years, especially on late timed populations

Sockeye

Information on Alaskan exploitation of Skeena sockeye comes primarily from the Northern Boundary Sockeye Run Reconstruction model, which uses estimated run-timing and migration route to provide estimates of interception rates in Alaska fisheries. We show time-series of Alaskan exploitation rates for each Skeena sockeye Conservation Unit with information (Figure 5) and the distribution of exploitation rates (Figure 6).
